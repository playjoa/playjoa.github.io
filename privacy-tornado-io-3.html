Privacy Policy

Tornado.io 3 - Battle Arena
Last Updated: November 12, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Tornado.io 3 - Battle Arena ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile game application Tornado.io 3 - Battle Arena (the "Game").

Tornado.io 3 - Battle Arena is a casual mobile game where you control a tornado that grows as it consumes objects in the environment. You can compete in battle arenas with other tornados controlled by AI or other players.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information that you provide directly to us, such as:

  • In-game progress and achievements
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Support requests and correspondence

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Game, we automatically collect certain information through Firebase and other services, including:

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • Game play information (levels completed, scores, time played, in-game progress)
  • Technical data (crash reports, performance data, app interactions)
  • Advertising identifiers for ad personalization
  • General location information (country/region level)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes, including:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Game
  • To save and sync your game progress across devices
  • To personalize your gaming experience
  • To display relevant advertisements
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize performance
  • To communicate with you about updates and support
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or technical issues
  • To understand how users interact with the Game

4. Third-Party Services

The Game uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services include:

  • Google Play Services / Apple Game Center - For game services and achievements
  • AdMob (Google) - For displaying advertisements
  • Firebase (Google) - For user data storage, analytics, crash reporting, and performance monitoring

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Privacy Information
  • Firebase Privacy Information

5. Data Storage and Firebase

We use Firebase services to store and manage user data, including:

  • Game progress and achievements
  • User preferences and settings
  • Analytics and usage data
  • Performance and crash data

Your data is stored securely on Google's Firebase servers. Firebase complies with major privacy regulations and employs industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

6. Advertisements

The Game displays third-party advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use information about your device and your use of the Game to provide personalized ads.

Information collected by AdMob may include:

  • Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android)
  • Device information and specifications
  • App usage and interaction data
  • General location information

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Toggle off "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

7. Children's Privacy

The Game is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at crossblackstudios@gmail.com, and we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We use Firebase's security features and follow industry best practices. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Game and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you stop using the Game, your data may be retained for a reasonable period for backup and analytical purposes.

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at crossblackstudios@gmail.com.
